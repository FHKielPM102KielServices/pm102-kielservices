About Us

Kiel, known as the ?Sailing city?, has become a tourist attraction for many, including international people. Due to the increasing number of International people (mainly students), it is difficult for them to get familiar with the surroundings/environment. People/Students who are new to the city, find it difficult to adjust to find their neighboring services, for example supermarkets, dormitories etc. As a solution to this problem, we are building a simple service based web application portal which can guide any person in the city. This web application will mainly include the following services: ? Hospitals ? Dormitories ? Supermarkets ? Transport services ? Entertainment services ? Shopping malls

  • Updates in current city
  • Weather Updates
  • Events for students
  • Entertainments
